Our Story

At PranaOS, we transform urban infrastructure into intelligent ecosystems. By combining porous concrete, IoT sensors and AI-driven analytics, we help cities monitor and protect the hidden health of urban soil and tree roots. Our technology reduces tree loss, prevents flooding, and lowers maintenance costs.
