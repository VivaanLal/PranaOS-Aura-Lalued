Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check my sensor health?

Navigate to the Dashboard and look for the 'System Health' status indicator on the top left.

Can I change the crop profile?

Yes, you can toggle between Grains, Vegetables, Orchards, and Pasture in the Dashboard settings.

What does a low PranaScore mean?

A low score typically indicates nutrient deficiency or waterlogging. Check the AI Recommendations panel for specific advice.

Our Story

At PranaOS, we transform urban infrastructure into intelligent ecosystems. By combining porous concrete, IoT sensors and AI-driven analytics, we help cities monitor and protect the hidden health of urban soil and tree roots. Our technology reduces tree loss, prevents flooding, and lowers maintenance costs.
